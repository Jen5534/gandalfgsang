● Perch Demo Script — Desk Allocation Flow

  Meet Sarah. It's Sunday evening and Perch is already working in the background. Earlier in the week Sarah used the Declare
  screen to mark Monday and Wednesday as office days. Overnight, the allocation engine picks that up, checks who else from her
  team is coming in, and quietly assigns her a desk — one that matches her profile preferences for a dual monitor and a spot
  near the window bank. By the time she wakes up Monday morning, she opens Perch on her phone and sees her allocation waiting on
   the My Allocation screen: Desk G-W1, Ground Floor, confirmed for today. One tap to confirm it, and it's locked in. No
  hunting, no first-come-first-served scramble.

  She arrives at the office, glances at the Floor Plan to orient herself, and can see exactly who else is in and where they're
  sitting. Her colleague checks Who's In to decide whether it's worth making the commute — half the team is already there, so
  it's a yes. That's Perch: the desk finds the person, not the other way around.
