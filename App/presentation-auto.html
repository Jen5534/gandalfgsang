Our Approach
No booking. Desks are automatically allocated on arrival.
Desk preferences — set once. Equipment needs, environment, and neighbourhood matched silently every time.
Arrival time — check in and the best available desk is allocated instantly. Earlier arrival, more choice.
User history — desks you've used before score higher, returning you to familiar spaces automatically.
No no-shows — nothing is held if you don't arrive. Capacity stays fluid for everyone.

Enterprise Security & Hosting
Zero Trust. Defence in depth. ~£133/month for 45,000 users.
Authentication
Azure AD SAML / OIDC, MFA enforced, Conditional Access blocks non-compliant devices
Authorisation (RBAC)
Three roles: User, Manager, Admin. OAuth 2.0 scopes. Principle of least privilege.
Encryption
TLS 1.3 in transit (HSTS). AES-256 at rest. Secrets in Key Vault — never in code.
Audit & GDPR
All events to Azure Monitor + Sentinel. GDPR: right-to-erasure, 13-month retention, UK data residency.
